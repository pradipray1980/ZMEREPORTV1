Details: This application shows the current status of any routing. It shows howmany SFCs are there at the operation, if there are work going on in that operation, it sows i there are SFCs on hold and number of NCs at the operation. 

The way to start the application 

URL/Dashboard/index.html?Router=<Router Name>&Site=<Site>
